Why does CivicLens exist?

CivicLens is dedicated to enhancing public participation in commenting on federal regulations. We collect data nightly from regulations.gov and build upon previous projects to provide guides, improved search functionality, and meaningful analysis. Using advanced AI models, we make document titles more readable, identify unique and representative comments, and analyze comment topics and sentiment to provide a clearer understanding of public feedback.

Discover more about our mission and methods on our About page.
